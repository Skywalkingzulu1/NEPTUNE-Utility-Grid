About Utility App

Crypto Market Trend Analyzer is a web app designed for crypto enthusiasts to track and analyze market trends and prices. The app uses real-time data from various sources, including CoinMarketCap and CryptoCompare, to provide users with a comprehensive view of the market. Users can set up alerts for specific cryptocurrencies, track price movements, and view historical data to make informed investment decisions.
